Take all the content from your favorite bicycle rides, routes,
community events, and tech tips.  Place them in an accessible
and open-source vehicle.  Replicate.  (this is an open [github repository](https://github.com/fullsendbikes/fullsendbikes)) <br>

We are a cycling community, and this resource exists to share, explore, and expand.  <br>
* This blog supports bicycle sales and consignment for [fullsendbikes.com](https://fullsendbikes.com/) <br>
* Our **[Knowledge Base](https://blog.fullsendbikes.com/page/kb/)** helps you find, adjust, recover or maintain your bicycle <br>
* Our **[Route Calendar](https://blog.fullsendbikes.com/ride-calendar/)** listes major bicycle events in Northern California <br>
* **[Local Routes](https://blog.fullsendbikes.com/page/routes/)** help you find your next favorite mountain, gravel or road route <br>
* **[Meetup](http://meetup.com/full-send-rides/)** manages our group ride schedules
* We have a **[Discord](https://discord.gg/GevP2Bgk8j)** group for local chat
* You can search for old articles or browse by **[Tags](http://localhost:1313/archives/)** 

**Welcome to Full Send Bikes Blog!**
