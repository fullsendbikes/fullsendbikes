## Let It Rip through Sugarloaf Mountain in San Mateo

This route is a quick, action-packed loop that should take about 30 to 45 minutes to conquer. Park down at 43 meters and power through a steady, rewarding climb up to the 178-meter (440' climb) peak before dropping into a fast, thrilling descent. <br>

* **Killer Summit Views:** The climb will definitely make you sweat, but the panoramic views of the Bay Area from the top of Sugarloaf Mountain are the ultimate payoff. <br>
* **Fun Trail Features:** On the descent, we'll hit the popular "Boost" singletrack—keep your eyes peeled for a sweet purpose-built wallride and a fun tree puzzle halfway down!<br>
* **Technical Downhills:** Get ready to fly. Trails like the "Slip" track offer fast, technical descents full of natural ruts and challenging root networks to keep you on your toes.<br>

## Dialing In Your Bike Setup

To get the absolute most out of this terrain, here is how you should prep your rig before riding:

* **Suspension:** A full-suspension mountain bike is your best friend here. Active travel will help you soak up the heavy roots and confidently carve downhill berms.
* **E-Bike Friendly:** Got an eMTB? Bring it! This network is incredibly e-bike friendly, and having a motor makes sessioning those steep trails and climbing back up a total breeze.
* **Tires & Weather:** Expect cool and slick conditions, so equip aggressive, mud-shedding tires and drop your PSI slightly to grip the slick, muddy terrain.
* **Heads Up:** Watch out for poison oak creeping onto the narrower singletrack, and note that there are temporary restrooms available at the trailhead while the main park facilities sometimes closed for service/repair.

Get your bikes tuned up, and let's go send it!
