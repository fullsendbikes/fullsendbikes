Best of Waterdog.  Avoids serious technical descents and ladders.  E-Bike accessible.<br>
Can be completed in just over an hour. <br>
Strava segments of note: [Labor of Love DH](https://www.strava.com/segments/17356374/local-legend?category=overall&athletes=all_athletes), [Enduro Descent #1](https://www.strava.com/segments/6824252/local-legend?category=overall&athletes=all_athletes), [Big Dog Love Loop](https://www.strava.com/segments/18582676/local-legend?category=overall&athletes=all_athletes).<br> 
Can be completed with a gravel bike, but not recommended. <br>
